Surgical Department

Surgical Services

Kisima's surgical department handles both minor procedures under local anaesthesia and major surgical interventions in a fully equipped, sterile operative theatre. Pre-operative assessment, intra-operative monitoring, and post-operative ward care are provided within the facility.

Theatre Capabilities
✓ Sterile instrument sets maintained to MoH standard
✓ Local and regional anaesthesia available
✓ Electrosurgical unit (diathermy) on-site
✓ Post-operative recovery and monitoring area
✓ Referral pathway with documentation for complex cases
Surgical Team
Surgical procedures are performed by qualified medical officers and surgeons. All cases are preceded by a formal pre-operative assessment. Post-operative care is managed in the inpatient ward with nursing support around the clock.
Minor Surgical Procedures

Incision & Drainage

Surgical drainage of abscesses, boils, and fluctuant swellings under local anaesthesia with sterile technique.

Minor

Wound Closure & Suturing

Primary repair of lacerations, layered closure, tension suturing, and debridement for traumatic wounds.

Minor

Circumcision

Male circumcision under local anaesthesia with dorsal slit or sleeve technique. Post-operative follow-up included.

Minor

Foreign Body Removal

Surgical extraction of embedded foreign bodies from soft tissue, ear canal, nose, and superficial ocular surface.

Minor

Excision of Lumps & Lesions

Excision of sebaceous cysts, lipomas, fibromas, and superficial benign lesions with histology referral if indicated.

Minor

Nail Avulsion

Ingrown toenail surgery (partial or total nail avulsion) and nail bed phenolisation to prevent recurrence.

Minor

Wound Debridement

Surgical removal of necrotic, infected, or foreign tissue from wounds, pressure ulcers, and infected sites.

Minor

Split-Thickness Skin Grafting

Application of split-thickness skin grafts for burns, post-excision defects, and non-healing ulcers.

Minor / Intermediate
Major Surgical Procedures

Caesarean Section

Emergency and elective CS for obstructed labour, fetal distress, cephalopelvic disproportion, placenta praevia, and repeat CS.

Major — Obstetric

Laparotomy

Exploratory and therapeutic laparotomy for acute abdomen, peritonitis, intestinal obstruction, and abdominal trauma.

Major — Abdominal

Appendicectomy

Open surgical removal of the appendix in acute appendicitis, perforated appendix, and appendiceal abscess.

Major — Abdominal

Hernia Repair

Inguinal, umbilical, and femoral hernia repair with mesh reinforcement or primary tissue repair based on presentation.

Major — General

Hydrocelectomy

Surgical correction of hydrocoele with excision and eversion of the tunica vaginalis or bottle-neck procedure.

Major — Urology

Myomectomy / Hysterectomy

Surgical management of symptomatic uterine fibroids via open myomectomy or total abdominal hysterectomy.

Major — Gynaecological

Ectopic Pregnancy Surgery

Emergency salpingectomy or salpingotomy for ruptured or unruptured ectopic pregnancy with haemoperitoneum management.

Major — Emergency Obstetric

Bowel Resection & Anastomosis

Resection of non-viable bowel with primary anastomosis or stoma formation in complex obstruction and strangulation.

Major — Gastrointestinal

Who We Are

About Kisima

Kisima Diagnostic and Treatment Centre is a Level 3A health facility in Ongata Rongai, Kajiado County. The centre was established to bridge the healthcare access gap in this rapidly growing community, offering a full continuum of primary, maternal, emergency, surgical, diagnostic, and specialist services to patients of all ages.

The facility operates without interruption — 24 hours a day, 365 days a year. Clinical care adheres to Kenya Ministry of Health Standard Treatment Guidelines and Level 3 facility protocols. No patient is turned away.

01
Always Open
No closures for weekends, public holidays, or any other day. Emergency and elective services run continuously around the clock.
02
Level 3A Certification
Accredited under Kenya MoH framework as a comprehensive primary care facility with full inpatient, maternity, and surgical capacity.
03
Integrated Services
Laboratory, pharmacy, surgical theatre, maternity, and outpatient services are co-located, enabling fast, coordinated patient care.
04
Community-Rooted
Located at the heart of Ongata Rongai, serving the health needs of families across Kajiado County with dignity and professionalism.

Conditions We Handle

Trauma & Injuries

Road traffic accidents, falls, lacerations, penetrating trauma, fractures, and crush injuries.

Obstetric Emergencies

Antepartum and postpartum haemorrhage, eclampsia, obstructed labour, and neonatal distress.

Cardiac & Respiratory

Chest pain, acute MI, severe asthma, respiratory failure, and cardiac arrhythmias.

Acute Surgical Conditions

Acute abdomen, suspected appendicitis, perforated viscus, and abscesses requiring drainage.

Paediatric Emergencies

Febrile convulsions, severe malaria, dehydration with shock, neonatal sepsis.

Poisoning & Overdose

Drug overdose, organophosphate poisoning, chemical ingestion, and toxic exposures.
